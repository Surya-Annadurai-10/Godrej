Recognition

Godrej Consumer Products is among the top 12 Indian companies to be featured on the Dow Jones Sustainability Emerging Markets Index 2019

Our Godrej Industries Chemicals (Valia) and Godrej Consumer Products (Malanpur) plants are rated GreenCo Gold under the Green Company Rating System for superior environmental sustainability

Our Godrej Agrovet Oil Palm Plantation (Chintampalli) and Godrej Chemicals (Ambernath) plants were awarded for energy efficiency at the CII National Awards 2020 for Excellence in Energy Management

Godrej Consumer Products is among the top 15 companies in India on the CDP Index and has received an A- rank for climate change. Godrej Industries Chemicals has received a B rank for climate change disclosures and water security transparency.
